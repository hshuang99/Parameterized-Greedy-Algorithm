\section{Quantum Circuits}
From the Enigma machine to von Neumann classical computer they all consists with wires and logic gates to execute the operations. Similary, quantum computer also need construct by those components but it is combine quantum wires and quantum logic gates to generate quantum circuits which used for transfer the quantum bits and execution the algorithm.

This is the ﬂesh and blood for quantum computer, and using these elemtns to handle the superposition state throguh Dirac notation:
