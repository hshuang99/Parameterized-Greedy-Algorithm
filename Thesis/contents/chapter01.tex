\section{Motivation}
As growing of quantum computer that their computation ability can hazard the current(classical) crypto-system. Such as Shor's algorithm can break the factoring problem, and Grover's Search algorithm can reduce length of security-bit of the symmetric cryptography.

On the other hand, quantum computer can bring new direction for design cryptography and cryptanalysis, we can think physically about computation, this is core spirit in quantum computaiton and quantum information. Speciﬁc in quantum cryptography, as the name suggests, it utilizes the cryptographic mechanisms underlying quantum circuits. The beneﬁts that using quantum mechanism is it provided quantum secrecy by uncertainty principle.

To improve efficiency for quantum circuits, it is essential to ﬁnd more compact implementation for construction. In quantum computing, many application heavily rely on linear reversible circuits; Therefore, deploying efficient methods to reduce the computational cost is key opint to providing more practical algorithms.

Currently, most symmetric cryptosystem are constructed by siginiﬁcant number of CNOT gates. Drawing inspiration from the ﬁeld of Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI), we aim to reduce quantum algorithm costs by focusing on circuit depth as our primary metric, targeting linear scaling.

In this work, we proposed an enhancement randomized greedy algorithm using heuristic methods to achieve the shallowest possible depth with minimum number of CNOT gates. our approach not only guarantees a speedup in the execution of quantum cryptographic algorithms but also reduces the impact of quantum noise and hardware constraints on the computation results.
